Xero Personas

Research-backed representations of the people and businesses we build for. Use them to align on who we're solving for and to test ideas with real-world context.

What are Personas?

They reflect patterns from customer research, interviews, and data—not single individuals, but the goals, pains, and behaviours that show up in our key segments. Use them as a shared, lightweight snapshot of the humans behind the work.

How to use them

  • In discovery & design — Reference personas in workshops and when prioritising features.
  • In testing — Recruit and run sessions with people who match a persona profile.
  • In storytelling — Use persona names and stories when presenting to stakeholders.
  • Chat with a persona — Try the "Chat with me" experience on each persona page (coming soon) to explore a Dynamic Persona in conversation.
  • Check out their Xero org — Try the "Visit my Xero" option on a persona page to view what their business looks like in Xero.
