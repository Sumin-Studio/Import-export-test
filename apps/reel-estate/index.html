    Recording settings

    Screen or tab

    Each time you preview or record, your browser asks which display, window, or tab to capture. That choice is only about the picture (and optional sound from that share) — not your microphone.

    This is not your mic. For narration, turn on My microphone on the Microphone tab. If the browser can’t attach audio to your share, you’ll only get video.

    Microphone

    Your voice from the computer’s mic—separate from any sound coming from the shared tab or screen.

    Mic

    Mic input level

    Webcam (picture-in-picture)

    Optional small circle of your camera in the bottom-right, similar to a video call.

    Camera

    Processed in the browser; the first time, a small model downloads from the internet (MediaPipe).

    Saved file format

    Container for the file you download after Stop recording. GIF is built from the recording (no sound). Does not change how the live preview looks.

    Format

    If MP4 isn’t available in this browser, the app saves WebM instead.
