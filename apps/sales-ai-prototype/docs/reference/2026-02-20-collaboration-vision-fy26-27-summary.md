# Collaboration Vision FY26-27 — Summary

**Source:** Collaboration Vision - FY26-27.pdf (Chong shared via Slack, 20 Feb 2026)  
**Status:** Working draft, Oct 2025. Marked sensitive.  
**Purpose:** Key points relevant to Bill AI — AB/SB collaboration, JAX, "do it together" pillar. Connects to Chong/Bharathi "AB and SB together" differentiation.

---

## Vision

**"The world's most collaborative small business platform"** — to save time for millions of SBs and ABs globally.

---

## Pillars

- **Do it together** — Small businesses, Accountants & bookkeepers, Employees
- **Do it for me**
- **Do it yourself**

---

## Core problem

**Requesting information** is the primary efficiency bottleneck for customers, resulting in lost time and revenue.

- Usually happens at the time of starting a Job
- **Request cycle:** Identify → Gather → Send → Follow up → Clarify → Receive
- On average, ABs spend XX hours per client per week requesting missing information

---

## Current state challenges

- **Prescriptive workflows:** Features like Ask and Jobs have rigid workflows, limiting flexibility
- **Disjointed functionality:** Notes, Discuss, Document attachments are isolated instances, lacking global visibility
- **Off-platform collaboration:** Happens in emails, SMS, phone — limited context, adds friction
- "Logging into Ask is prohibitive, especially when clients have multiple Xeros" — AB

---

## Five principles (future state)

| Principle | Description |
|-----------|-------------|
| **Anywhere** | Collaborative tools wherever SBs and ABs are |
| **Connected** | Collaborative functionality shouldn't exist in isolation |
| **Contextual** | Provide context for communications |
| **Proactive** | Provide proactive and suggested actions |
| **Efficient** | Supercharge productivity by unblocking AB/SB do-it-together workflows |

---

## JAX in collaboration context

- **AI creates tasks:** JAX translates comments into tasks and provides apt task description based on content
- **Summarise threads:** JAX can summarise ephemeral comments and create more permanent notes for auditing
- **Smart replies:** JAX generates preview reply to common requests, provides choice on tone per customer
- **Work history:** Track changes by ABs, SBs, JAX and 3rd party agents — "never miss a thing"

---

## Evolution

**From Human-Driven to AI-Powered workflows:**
1. In-Product Collaboration
2. AI-Optimized Tasks
3. AI-Driven Collaboration
4. Automated Agent-to-Agent

Human out of the loop and things are just happening. Human brought in by exception for approvals.
