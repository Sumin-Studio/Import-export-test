### Working summary (until full sync)

- **What it is:** Just Pay is the **“make it easy to pay”** agent, focused on simplifying how bills actually get paid once decisions are made. It leans into quick, confident payment execution rather than cashflow planning or deep risk analysis.
- **Relationship to the other agents:**
  - **Bill Runner**: decides *which* bills to pay and *when* based on cash flow.
  - **Safety Shield / Risk Radar**: decides *which bills deserve a second look* before money moves.
  - **Just Pay**: makes it **fast and low-friction to actually pay** the approved bills.
- **Customer value:** Reduce the friction between “I’ve decided to pay this” and “the money has actually moved” — fewer steps across bank portals, less double entry, and a clearer, more confident experience inside Xero.
