# Agents design sync — ritual (Confluence-ready)

**Overview:** Short definition of an optional "Agents design sync" for the payments agents squads. Copy this content into a Confluence page and link it from the Payments Agents Hub or PROJECT-STATUS so the team can find it. Adjust cadence and scope to fit.

---

## Purpose

A regular time for designers (and product/eng as needed) working on Bill Cashflow, Safety Shield, Approval Automation, and related agent streams to share work in progress, cross-pollinate, and align on components and patterns. Complements the existing **Payments & Eco Regional Crit**; this sync is focused on the agents work specifically.

## Cadence

- **Suggested:** Fortnightly, or align with the existing Regional Crit (e.g. one frame or block per sync).
- **Duration:** 30–45 minutes.
- **Who’s in scope:** Squad leads and designers (and optionally PM/eng) for the four agents squads; extend to others (e.g. Brett, Thomas) when pattern or Xerocon story is on the agenda.

## What to bring

- **Figma or Confluence link** to the work you want feedback on.
- **2–3 specific questions** (e.g. “Does this evidence pattern feel trustworthy?” “Where should this live: list, detail, or Spotlight?”).
- Optional: add a card or screenshot to the **Miro board** (if you use one) and link from there. See [Miro — Agents status / Xerocon board](2026-03-10-miro-agents-status-xerocon-board.md).

## Structure (suggestion)

- **5–10 min:** Quick tour of the board or status (what’s in progress across squads).
- **Remaining time:** 1–2 deep dives (same format as Regional Crit: context, quiet feedback, discussion) or open Q&A.
