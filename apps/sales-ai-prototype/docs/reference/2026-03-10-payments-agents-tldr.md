## Payments Agents — TL;DR (Bill Runner, Safety Shield / Risk Radar, Just Pay)

**Date:** 2026-03-10  
**Scope:** Bill Runner, Safety Shield / “Risk Radar”, and Just Pay  

### What we’re building

- **Bill Runner** — an AI agent that looks at upcoming bills, expected money in, and a customer’s cash buffer, then proposes a **90‑day payment plan**. It tells you which bills to pay when, so you can stay solvent without living in spreadsheets.
- **Safety Shield / “Risk Radar”** — a **background risk and anomaly layer** for bills. It scans bills and supplier history to find things that deserve a second look (duplicates, first‑time suppliers with big amounts, abnormal spikes, suspicious changes) and explains *why* it paused the flow.
- **Just Pay** — the **fast path to actually moving money** once decisions are made. It focuses on making approved payments feel simple and confident, rather than bouncing between Xero and bank portals.

Together, these agents shift bills from a manual, bill‑by‑bill workflow into an **exception‑driven, plan‑driven system**: Bill Runner plans, Safety Shield protects, Just Pay executes.

### Why we’re doing it

- Today, only a small fraction of customers create and pay bills in Xero because it feels like **extra admin work** on top of everything else.
- Owners and finance leads constantly juggle **“which bills can I safely pay this week?”**, **“will I run out of cash?”**, and **“am I about to pay the wrong thing twice?”** using ad‑hoc spreadsheets and bank portals.
- The vision is to move from Xero as a **system of record** (what happened) to a **system of action** (what to do next), starting with bills and cash out.

### Customer value in plain language

- **Less stress about cash flow:** Bill Runner turns messy mental math into a clear, explainable 90‑day plan.
- **Safer approvals:** Safety Shield / “Risk Radar” catches suspicious or unusual bills early, so customers are less likely to pay duplicates or miss obvious red flags.
- **Simpler payment execution:** Just Pay removes friction between “approved” and “paid”, so more of the work happens inside Xero instead of scattered across tools.

### One‑liner for Safety Shield / “Risk Radar”

> A shared **risk radar for bills** that quietly watches what’s coming through, flags the ones that look wrong or unusual, and tells you why before money moves.
