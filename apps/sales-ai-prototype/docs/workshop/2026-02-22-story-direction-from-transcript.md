# Story direction from transcript (22 Feb 2026)

**Source:** Jon walking/thinking out loud about the concept demos. Captured from transcript.

**Purpose:** How the story should go for the three swim lanes — interaction models and where each experience lives.

---

## Key ideas

### Swim Lane 1 — Bill Pay Planner

- **3-click path on the dashboard, NOT in the chatbot.** Show status upfront, progressive disclosure on tap, conservative/standard/growth choice, checkboxes, pay.
- "Find a way to get it out of the chatbot" — SL1 could work as a **specialized bill screen** that gathers things up, **no JAX needed**.

### Swim Lane 2 — Approval / stress → suggestion

- Could be a **pure ChatGPT integration story**: e.g. "I'm stressed about money this week" → AI suggests low-priority bills to clear.

### Swim Lane 3 — Just Pay

- Maybe **in the chatbot**, then you **receive a WhatsApp message**. "That's really powerful."

### Three distinct interaction models

1. **Dashboard-native** — SL1: do it in the bills screen, no chat required.
2. **Conversational AI** — SL2 (and/or SL3 entry): ChatGPT-style, natural language in, suggestion or action out.
3. **Proactive notification** — SL3: you get a WhatsApp (or similar) ping; approve or act from there.
