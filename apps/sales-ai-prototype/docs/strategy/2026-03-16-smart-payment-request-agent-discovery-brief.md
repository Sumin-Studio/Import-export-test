# Smart Payment Request Agent -- 4-Week Discovery Brief

**Sponsor:** Darren Alvares (Head of Product -- Payments Growth)
**Discovery Lead:** Johnathan Rivera (Senior PM)
**Duration:** 4 weeks (10 March -- 4 April 2026)
**Status:** Kickoff

## Why are we doing this?

Small business owners spend 15+ hours a week on financial admin. Xero's payment request creation is manual, fragmented, and places cognitive load on the merchant. As we introduce more ways to request payment (quotes, deposits, progress payments, payment plans, invoices, payment links), the complexity only grows.

The Smart Payment Request Agent (Pillar 2 of the Payments Agent Vision) is an intelligent agent that analyses business data to automatically draft the right financial document -- Quote, Invoice, Deposit, or Payment Link -- for each transaction.

This discovery is not about committing to build. It's about identifying where the highest-value thin slice sits.

## 4-Week Plan
- **Week 1 -- Frame & Align:** Kickoff, map existing landscape, review existing research, catalogue capabilities, connect with adjacent workstreams
- **Week 2 -- Customer & Data Deep Dive:** 5-8 customer interviews, quantitative analysis, competitive scan, identify top 2-3 thin-slice opportunities
- **Week 3 -- Solution Hypotheses & Feasibility:** 2-3 solution concepts, technical feasibility, risk assessment, rough effort estimation, early concept validation
- **Week 4 -- Synthesise & Recommend:** Discovery Playback with recommended thin-slice, proposed solution approach, capability dependencies, estimated investment, open questions

## Guiding Principles
- Learn, don't build
- Start from the customer job, not the technology
- Leverage what exists
- Think thin, ship fast
- Respect merchant trust -- assume human-in-the-loop for send; automation of drafting, accounting, and scheduling is where early wins are
