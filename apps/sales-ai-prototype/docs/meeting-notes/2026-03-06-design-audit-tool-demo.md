# Design Audit Tool Demo & Feedback

**Date:** 2026-03-06
**Context:** Meeting with design audit tool startup (Stanford founders)

## Product Demo & Initial Feedback

- Tested their design audit tool with Figma prototype
- Major friction points identified:
  - "Only root level sections supported" error blocked initial attempt
  - Required form completion before seeing value
  - No drag-and-drop for screenshots
  - Mandatory login before demo
- Recommendation: Show value in <10 seconds without signup
  - Allow 5-10 free trials before requiring account
  - Enable direct paste from clipboard for screenshots

## Company Background & Vision

- Founded by Stanford roommates inspired by "Creativity Inc" (Pixar methodology)
- CEO: Former Google PM (AdWords optimization) and Apple/Siri product experience
- Laura: Founding designer on team
- Mission: Give creative people seat at table, connect design to business outcomes
- Two main use cases:
  1. Design system compliance (revenue driver)
  2. Product optimization through behavioral science

## Designer Productivity Challenges at Xero

- 100+ designer organization with separate design systems team
- Jon's role: Principal designer handling "weird things" - roving problem solver
- Key pain points designers face:
  - Defensive crouch due to AI disruption fears
  - Pressure to show craft vs AI-generated work
  - Need to prove design decisions to stakeholders
  - Lack confidence presenting to leadership

## AI Integration Success Story

- Recent prototype: Cash flow agent for small business bill prioritization
- Went from "no way in hell" to green light in one demo
- Challenge: Design team questioned lack of design system compliance
- Solution approach: 99 AI-generated concepts with synthetic user quotes
  - Themed feedback on potential user complaints
  - Showed strategic thinking beyond just prototyping

## Design Storytelling & Stakeholder Buy-in

- Core issue: Designers struggle to "show their work"
- Head of Design (Shay) feedback: Get to the point faster
  - Wants 5-second comparison: current vs proposed
  - Values confidence over lengthy process documentation
- Synthetic user testing emerging as validation method
  - Not replacement for real users, but efficient iteration tool
  - Enables 14 rounds of iteration in 2 days before real user testing

## Product Positioning Recommendations

- Target: Selling "confidence" to designers
- Key value prop: Audit trail explaining why designs work
- Comparison to Pepsi logo redesign PDF - need substance not BS
- Success metric: Enable designers to answer stakeholder questions confidently
- Potential JavaScript integration for automatic screen recording analysis
  - Jon comfortable with implementation
  - Would need company security approval
