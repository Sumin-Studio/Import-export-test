## The Cash Crunch Problem

When a business can't pay their bills, they have limited options:

1. Delay paying their bills
2. Pull in an invoice
3. Get a short-term loan / apply for a credit card
4. Pay a larger bill and accept late fees
5. Choose not to pay yourself

## Three Layered Cake Metaphor

An escalating autonomy model for how the product helps:

| Level | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Insight** | Surface the information |
| **Sweeten to taste** | Let users adjust and customise |
| **Set and forget?** | Automation handles it |
| **We gotcha fam** | Fully autonomous |
