## Bill Pay Agent Concept

- Average customer has **40 bills to pay monthly** (medium/high-end SKUs have 50-100 bills)
- Current process: go to awaiting payment tab, click each bill individually

**Proposed pay planner agent** leverages existing data:
- Invoice payments coming in
- Bank reconciliation records
- Payroll information
- Complete money in/money out understanding

Agent could provide pre-built payment plans based on:
- Due dates and critical suppliers (landlords, etc.)
- Cash flow projections
- User-defined goals (pay all bills vs. maintain cash buffer)

**Tangible claims:** save time, maintain control, augment user intuition
