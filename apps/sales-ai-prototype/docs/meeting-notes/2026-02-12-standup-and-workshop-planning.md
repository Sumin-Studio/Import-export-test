## 5. Bill pay agent concept (pay planner)

- **Scale:** Average customer ~40 bills/month to pay; medium/high SKUs 50–100.
- **Current pain:** Awaiting payment tab → click each bill individually.
- **Pay planner agent** could use existing data:
  - Invoice payments in, bank rec, payroll → full money in/out view.
- **Outputs:** Pre-built payment plans from due dates, critical suppliers (e.g. landlords), cash flow projections, user goals (pay all vs maintain buffer).
- **Claims:** Save time, maintain control, augment intuition.
