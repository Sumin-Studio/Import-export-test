### Patterns & themes

- "Surface AI insights in context where customers already work (Spotlight, Quick view, dashboards) using consistent patterns."
- "Use JAX as an optional drill‑down panel, not an auto‑popping Clippy; keep the human firmly in the loop."
- "Balance visibility of risk against UI real estate—assume risky bills are rare and avoid noisy, always‑on columns."
- "Reuse a common surfacing → trigger → JAX pattern across bills, tax and payroll rather than inventing one‑offs."
- "Invest in modern experiences like Quick view and redesigned bills pages instead of deepening legacy screens."
- "Nail the ‘boring basics’ of automation (extraction, duplicates, PO matching, learned behaviours) as the trust foundation for more advanced AI."
