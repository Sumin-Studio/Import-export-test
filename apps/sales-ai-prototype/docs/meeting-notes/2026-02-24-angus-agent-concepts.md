## Presentation Strategy for Diya

### The Challenge
- Brett encouraged more ambitious thinking vs incremental improvements
- Proposed: 3 iPhone screens showing completed AI actions
  - Screen 1: "We paid everybody" with one exception highlighted
  - Screen 2: Tap exception → "We weren't sure about this one"
  - Screen 3: Explanation of decision-making process
- Results-first approach harder for audiences to accept
- Need air cover for ambitious positioning

### Audience Calibration
- **Knowledgeable audiences** can fill gaps themselves → sparse version works
- **Uninformed audiences** need explicit details "painted in big red strokes" → detailed version
- **Recommendation:** Prepare both sparse and detailed versions
