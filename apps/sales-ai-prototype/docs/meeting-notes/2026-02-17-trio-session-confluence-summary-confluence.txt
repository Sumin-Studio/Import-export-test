h2. Outcome

After sorting through the ideas (gut check on concepts, swim lane alignment), the team landed on *one prototype* for Friday — not three. Focus is on one high-priority proof of concept to drive discussion.

----

h2. Scope and focus

* *Swim lane:* Focus is *Swim lane 1 — Cash-flow aware planner* (bill preparation and planning, latter part of the bill journey). Swim lane 2 (approval) was deprioritised because of overlap with Melio's out-of-the-box payment approval.
* *Geography:* *US only* for now. PoC design is flexible enough to pivot to UK if API integration is easier there; Melio's iframe/SDK structure was noted as a potential constraint.
* *Framing:* Treat the prototype as a *proof of concept to drive discussion*. Goal is "what does good look like" and productive conversation with Melio/stakeholders — even if we get shot down, it drives the next iteration.

----

h2. Concepts: what stayed in vs out

*Retained (in scope for the one prototype):*
Cash runway watcher, Instant Pay intent capture, Early pay discount radar, Bill timing optimizer (discounts may be de-emphasised).

*Dismissed for this round:*
Policy-aware approval drafts, Risk tier routing (just logic), One-Tap Billets Pay (retrospective bills — confusion), Approval SLA nudger, Multi-account Liquidity Splitter.
